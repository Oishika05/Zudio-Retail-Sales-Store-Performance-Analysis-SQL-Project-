# Zudio-Retail-Sales-Store-Performance-Analysis-SQL-Project-
📊 **Project Overview**

This project analyzes retail sales, store performance, product demand, and customer behavior for a Zudio-like fashion retail business using SQL.

The goal is to convert raw transactional retail data into a structured relational database and perform analytical queries to identify sales trends, high-performing stores, profitable product categories, and customer purchasing patterns.

🎯 **Business Objectives**

Transform raw retail data into normalized relational tables

Analyze store performance and profitability

Identify top-performing products and categories

Understand customer purchasing behavior

Evaluate sales trends across months and regions

Support strategic decisions for inventory, marketing, and store expansion

🔍 **Key Insights**
Sales Performance

October and November are peak sales months, indicating festive demand.

Sales fluctuate seasonally with peaks in April, August, and October.

Regional Performance

Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, and Delhi generate the highest profits.

Kolkata, Mysuru, and Jammu contribute the most revenue among cities.

Product Demand

Dresses, T-shirts, Jackets, and Skirts dominate sales volume.

Some Kids and Women category products show very low sales, suggesting potential discontinuation.

Customer Insights

A small group of customers contributes disproportionately to revenue.

Only 29 repeat customers, indicating opportunity for loyalty programs.

Store Performance

Rented stores outperform owned stores, likely due to better locations.

Store IDs 13, 68, and 4 generate the highest profits.

📈 **Business Recommendations**

Leverage Seasonal Demand
Increase inventory and marketing during festive months.

Improve Customer Retention
Implement loyalty programs and personalized promotions.

Optimize Product Portfolio
Focus on high-demand clothing categories.

Expand High-Performing Regions
Invest more in top-revenue states and cities.

Increase Order Value
Use bundling and cross-selling strategies.

📌 **Outcome**

This project demonstrates how SQL-based retail analytics can help businesses identify growth opportunities, optimize product portfolios, improve customer retention, and enhance store performance.
